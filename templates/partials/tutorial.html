Candy Guide

Learn the basics of boosters, matches, and special tiles.

Boosters

The shop sells three tactical powerups that can be used mid-level.

  • Shuffle Rearrange all candies
  • Switch Swap two adjacent cells without a match
  • Bomb Clear a 4x4 area of your choice

Matches

Bombs are activated by matching the right color. There are 5 different colors and the match has to be of the same color.

  • Match 3 Clear the candies and score points.
  • Match 4 Creates a line bomb.
  • Match 5 Creates a large blast bomb.
  • L or T Creates a medium blast bomb.
  • Square (2x2) Creates a small blast bomb.

Game Mechanics

The score multiplier rises after strong moves, drops after weak or empty moves, and applies to every point you earn.

Obstacles

  • Hard candies Adjacent matches crack them before they can be cleared.
  • Sugar chests Adjacent matches crack them until they burst into sugar coins.
  • Blocker generators Every few matches they create new hard candies nearby.

Modes

  • Level Mode Finish goals with limited moves.
  • Blocker Mode Survive as hard candies spread and chase a high score.
  • Time Mode Beat the clock as it speeds up; matches add time.
